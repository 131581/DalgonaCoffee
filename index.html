Dalgona Coffee Recipe

This yummy South korean coffee treat that's all over the internet require only four ingredients to make - all of which you probably have in the cupboard - you feel like you've won the jackpot,right?

Ingredients

  • Instant coffe powder or expresso granules
  • Hot water
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Dalgona Making Process

Step 1: Combine the instant coffee, sugar and hot water
Add 2 tablespoon(tbsp) of instant coffee, 2tbsp of sugar and 2tbsp of hot water to a medium mixing bowl
Step 2: whisk, whisk, whisk until the coffee is light and fluffy
using an electric hand-held mixer, whip the coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy and holds stiff peaks when the whisk is removed.
Step 3: Serve with Milk
Use a spoon to garnish a glass of hot or cold milk with the coffee cream serve with a starw and stir,stir,stir to enjoy!
